GIVING A TESTIMONY ON WEDNESDAY EVENING

The church now has live-streaming capability, which enables both in person and remote attendance. Neither live-stream nor Zoom can be used to give testimonies because there are feedback and looping problems.

The ONLY WAY to give a testimony now is to call in by using your phone.

INSTRUCTIONS: Use these if you are listening on your computer or iPad:

FIRST. Call this number on your phone: 603-224-0818. An usher will answer your call. For Zoom listeners on your phone: You will need to leave the Zoom meeting temporarily to dial in.

SECOND. Give your name to the usher and tell him or her that you want to share a testimony.

THIRD. Wait for the usher to say it is your turn to speak; then turn down the sound on your computer or device. This is important; otherwise, the sound from your device will interfere with your testimony and you will not be understood.

FOURTH. Please state your name and share your testimony.

FIFTH. When finished, hang up. You may now turn up the volume on your computer or other device. For Zoom listeners: Please join meeting again on your phone. The host will let you in.

To give a testimony on Wednesday, you can email clerk@christianscienceconcordnh.org to submit a testimony in advance to be read during the service.
